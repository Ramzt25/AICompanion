AI Companion - Embeddable Widget Demo

This page demonstrates how the AI Companion can be embedded into any website as a floating assistant.

Integration Code:

<script src="http://localhost:3001/widget.js" data-org-id="demo-org-id" data-user-id="demo-user"></script>

Features:

  • Floating chat widget that stays out of the way
  • Auto-minimizes after 30 seconds of inactivity
  • Access to knowledge base, analytics, and AI features
  • Contextual assistance based on current page content
  • No iframe - direct DOM injection for better performance

The AI Assistant should appear in the bottom-right corner of this page.
